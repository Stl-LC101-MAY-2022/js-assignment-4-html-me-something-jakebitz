About Us

Who do we affect?

What is our Guiding Mindset?

At RJM, we understand that crime, trauma, and mental health diseases are the symptoms of more complex and systemic issues. Though we aim to temper these symptoms, our goal is to address the root causes. More specifically, we help people help themselves by providing them with the physical, social, and educational tools needed to build productive self-sustaining outcomes.
